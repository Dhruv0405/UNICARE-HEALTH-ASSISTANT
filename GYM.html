Back Exercises

Lat Pulldown

Equipment: Cable Machine

Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 12 reps

Description: Sit at a lat pulldown machine with your thighs secured. Grab the bar with a wide grip, lean back slightly, and pull the bar down to your chest while squeezing your shoulder blades together. Slowly return to the starting position.

Seated Row

Equipment: Cable Machine

Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Description: Sit at a seated row machine with your feet on the platform and knees slightly bent. Pull the handle toward your lower abdomen while keeping your back straight. Squeeze your shoulder blades and slowly return to the starting position.

Barbell Row

Equipment: Barbell

Sets & Reps: 4 sets of 8-10 reps

Description: Bend at your hips and knees, holding a barbell with an overhand grip. Keep your back flat and pull the barbell to your lower ribcage, squeezing your shoulder blades together. Lower the weight with control.

T-Bar Row

Equipment: T-Bar Row Machine

Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 10 reps

Description: Position yourself on the T-bar row machine with your chest against the pad. Grab the handles and pull the weight toward your chest, focusing on retracting your shoulder blades. Lower the weight with control.

Pull-ups

Equipment: Pull-up Bar

Sets & Reps: 3 sets of max reps

Description: Hang from a pull-up bar with hands slightly wider than shoulder-width. Pull your body up until your chin clears the bar, then lower yourself with control. Focus on using your back muscles rather than your arms.

Leg Exercises

Barbell Squat

Equipment: Barbell, Squat Rack

Sets & Reps: 4 sets of 8-10 reps

Description: Position a barbell across your upper back and shoulders. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart and squat down until your thighs are parallel to the ground. Push through your heels to return to the starting position.

Leg Press

Equipment: Leg Press Machine

Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 12 reps

Description: Sit on the leg press machine with your feet shoulder-width apart on the platform. Release the safety and lower the platform by bending your knees. Push through your heels to extend your legs without locking your knees.

Leg Extension

Equipment: Leg Extension Machine

Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 15 reps

Description: Sit on the leg extension machine with your ankles against the lower pad. Extend your knees to lift the weight, then lower it back with control. Focus on contracting your quadriceps throughout the movement.

Lying Leg Curl

Equipment: Leg Curl Machine

Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 12 reps

Description: Lie face down on the leg curl machine with the pad against your ankles. Curl your legs up by bending your knees, bringing your heels toward your buttocks. Lower the weight with control.

Standing Calf Raise

Equipment: Calf Raise Machine

Sets & Reps: 4 sets of 15-20 reps

Description: Stand on the calf raise machine with the balls of your feet on the platform. Lower your heels as far as possible, then raise up onto your toes as high as you can. Focus on a full range of motion and controlled movement.

Biceps Exercises

Barbell Curl

Equipment: Barbell

Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Description: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart holding a barbell with an underhand grip. Keep your upper arms stationary and curl the weight toward your shoulders. Lower the barbell with control.

Dumbbell Curl

Equipment: Dumbbells

Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 12 reps

Description: Stand holding dumbbells at your sides with palms facing forward. Keep your upper arms stationary and curl the weights toward your shoulders. Lower the dumbbells with control.

Preacher Curl

Equipment: Preacher Bench, EZ Bar

Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 10 reps

Description: Sit at a preacher bench with your upper arms resting on the pad. Hold an EZ bar with an underhand grip and curl the weight up. Lower the bar with control, fully extending your arms.

Cable Curl

Equipment: Cable Machine

Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 15 reps

Description: Stand in front of a cable machine with a straight bar attachment set to the lowest position. Grip the bar with palms up and curl the bar toward your shoulders while keeping your upper arms stationary.

Hammer Curl

Equipment: Dumbbells

Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 12 reps

Description: Stand holding dumbbells at your sides with palms facing your body. Keep your upper arms stationary and curl the weights toward your shoulders while maintaining the neutral grip. Lower with control.

Triceps Exercises

Tricep Pushdown

Equipment: Cable Machine

Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Description: Sit facing a cable machine with a straight or V-bar attachment at upper chest height. Grab the bar with an overhand grip and push it down until your arms are fully extended. Slowly return to the starting position.

Skull Crushers

Equipment: EZ Bar, Bench

Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 10 reps

Description: Lie on a bench holding an EZ bar with an overhand grip. Extend your arms directly above your chest, then bend your elbows to lower the bar toward your forehead. Extend your arms to return to the starting position.

Tricep Dips

Equipment: Dip Station or Bench

Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Description: Position yourself on parallel bars with arms extended. Lower your body by bending your elbows until your upper arms are parallel to the floor. Push yourself back up to the starting position.

Overhead Tricep Extension

Equipment: Dumbbell or Cable

Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 12 reps

Description: Stand or sit holding a dumbbell with both hands. Raise the dumbbell overhead, then lower it behind your head by bending your elbows. Extend your arms to raise the dumbbell back overhead.

Close Grip Bench Press

Equipment: Barbell, Bench

Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

Description: Lie on a bench holding a barbell with hands shoulder-width apart or closer. Lower the bar to your lower chest, then press it back up by extending your arms. Keep your elbows close to your body throughout.

Abs Exercises

Cable Crunch

Equipment: Cable Machine, Rope Attachment

Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 15 reps

Description: Kneel in front of a cable machine with a rope attachment at a high position. Hold the rope behind your head and crunch down, bringing your elbows toward your knees. Slowly return to the starting position.

Hanging Leg Raise

Equipment: Pull-up Bar

Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 12 reps

Description: Hang from a pull-up bar with your arms fully extended. Keeping your legs straight, raise them until they form a 90-degree angle with your torso. Lower your legs with control.

Decline Sit-up

Equipment: Decline Bench

Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 15 reps

Description: Secure your feet on a decline bench and lie back. Cross your arms over your chest or place hands behind your head. Sit up by flexing your abs to bring your chest toward your knees. Lower yourself with control.

Russian Twist

Equipment: Weight Plate or Medicine Ball

Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 20 twists (10 each side)

Description: Sit on the floor with knees bent and feet elevated. Hold a weight with both hands and lean back slightly. Twist your torso to bring the weight to one side, then the other. Keep your core engaged throughout.

Ab Rollout

Equipment: Ab Wheel

Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 10 reps

Description: Kneel on the floor holding an ab wheel with both hands. Roll the wheel forward, extending your body as far as you can while keeping your core engaged. Use your abs to pull yourself back to the starting position.

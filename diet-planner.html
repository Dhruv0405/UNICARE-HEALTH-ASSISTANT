Balanced Diet

A balanced diet includes proteins, carbohydrates, fats, vitamins, and minerals in the right proportions to maintain good health.

Hydration

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily to stay hydrated. Water helps in digestion, absorption, and transportation of nutrients.
